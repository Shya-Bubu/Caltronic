# Exam Guide: Q3 & Q4 Solution Techniques

## Q3 (Graphical)
- Read hfe: ΔIC/ΔIB at constant VCE
- Read hoe: slope of IB curve at Q-point
- DC load line: x-int = VCC, slope = −1/(RC+RE)
- AC load line: passes through Q, slope = −1/RAC
- Waveform: read VCE at IB extremes along AC load line

## Q4 (Two-Stage CE-CC)
- Stage 1 (CE): use full h-param formulas with hoe
- Stage 2 (CC): Av ≈ 1, very high Rin, very low Rout
- Combined: Av = Av1(loaded) × Av2, Rin = Rin1, Rout = Rout2
