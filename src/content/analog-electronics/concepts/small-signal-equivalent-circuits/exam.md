## Common Mistakes
- **Forgetting VCC = AC ground**: This is the #1 mistake. The top of RC and R1 must connect to ground in the AC model.
- **Leaving bypass capacitors as open**: Bypass caps are short for AC! If CE bypasses RE, then RE disappears from the AC circuit.
- **Not including RL**: The coupling capacitor connects RL to the output. RL appears in parallel with RC.
- **Drawing RC + RL in series instead of parallel**: Both connect from collector to ground → parallel, not series.
