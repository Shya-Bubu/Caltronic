# Exam Guide: Multi-Stage Amplifiers and Feedback

## Typical Questions
| Pattern | Marks | Time |
|---------|-------|------|
| **Find overall gain of 2-stage amplifier** | 6-8 | 6 min |
| **Calculate Rin/Rout of combined circuit** | 4-6 | 4 min |
| **Identify feedback topology** | 3-4 | 2 min |
| **Draw circuit with feedback loading** | 4-5 | 4 min |

## Common Mistakes
- **Using unloaded gains for cascade**: Always use loaded gains (RC1||Rin2 for stage 1).
- **Wrong Rin/Rout for combined circuit**: Rin = first stage, Rout = last stage. Don't confuse them.
- **Feedback topology confusion**: Check where Rf connects at BOTH output (V or I sampling) AND input (series or shunt mixing).
- **Forgetting biasing R in inter-stage loading**: R1||R2 of stage 2 also loads stage 1 (in parallel with Rin,base2).
