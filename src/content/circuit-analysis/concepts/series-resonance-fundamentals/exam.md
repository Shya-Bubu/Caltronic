# Exam Guide: Series Resonance Fundamentals

## Typical Questions
| Pattern | Marks | Time |
|---------|-------|------|
| **Calculate ω₀ from L and C** | 2-3 | 2 min |
| **Find I, VR, VL, VC at resonance** | 5-8 | 6 min |
| **Determine circuit behaviour (capacitive/inductive) at given ω** | 3-4 | 3 min |
| **Sketch |Z| vs ω curve** | 4-5 | 4 min |

## Common Mistakes
- **Using f instead of ω**: ω₀ = 1/√LC gives rad/s. For Hz: f₀ = ω₀/(2π). Don't mix them up.
- **Thinking VL + VC = 0 means each is 0**: They cancel as phasors, but each can be Q × Vs individually.
- **Forgetting units**: L in Henries, C in Farads. mH = 10⁻³ H, μF = 10⁻⁶ F.
