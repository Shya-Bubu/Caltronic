## Common Mistakes
- **Arithmetic vs geometric mean**: ω₀ = √(ω₁ω₂), NOT (ω₁+ω₂)/2. The symmetric approximation only works for high Q.
- **−3dB ≠ half current**: −3dB means half POWER. Current drops to 1/√2 (0.707), not 1/2.
- **B = R/L, not L/R**: Units check: [Ω]/[H] = [Ω]/[Ω·s] = [1/s] = [rad/s]. ✓
