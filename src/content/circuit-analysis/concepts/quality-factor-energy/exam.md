## Common Mistakes
- **Mixing up series/parallel Q**: Series: Q = ωoL/R. Parallel: Q = ωoRC = R/ωoL. These are NOT the same formula!
- **Forgetting 2π in energy definition**: Q = **2π** × wS/wD, not just wS/wD.
- **Underrating components**: VC = Q×Vs can be >> Vs. Always check component voltage/current ratings against Q×source.
