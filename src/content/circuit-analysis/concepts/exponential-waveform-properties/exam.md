## Common Mistakes
- **Rise time formula**: t_r = 2.2τ, NOT 2τ or 2.3τ. Exact: τ·ln(9) = 2.197τ.
- **Elapsed time sign**: The argument of ln must be positive. Check that numerator and denominator have the same sign.
- **Confusing 63% and 37%**: After 1τ, 63% COMPLETE (not 63% remaining). The remaining fraction is 37%.
