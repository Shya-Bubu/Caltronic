## Common Mistakes
- **Z is maximum (not minimum) at parallel resonance**: Series: Z = R (min). Parallel: Z = R (max).
- **Parallel Q formula**: Q = ωoRC (not ωoL/R). Or equivalently Q = R/ωoL.
- **B = 1/RC (not R/C or RC)**: Double check units: [1/(ΩF)] = [1/(Ω·s)] = [rad/s]. ✓
