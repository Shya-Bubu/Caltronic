## Common Mistakes
- **A + 1 = A** (WRONG! A + 1 = 1). Remember: OR with 1 always gives 1.
- **Forgetting the second distributive law**: A + BC = (A+B)(A+C) is uniquely Boolean.
- **De Morgan's sign swap**: You must BOTH complement each variable AND swap the operator.
