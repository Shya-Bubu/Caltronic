# Exam Guide: Combinational Circuit Design

## Typical Questions
| Pattern | Marks | Time |
|---------|-------|------|
| **Write SOP/POS from truth table** | 4-6 | 5 min |
| **Design circuit from requirement** | 8-10 | 10 min |
| **Identify minterms/maxterms** | 3-4 | 3 min |
| **Draw three-layer circuit** | 5-7 | 6 min |

## Common Mistakes
- **Complementation in minterms**: Variable is complemented when its value is **0** (not 1).
- **Complementation in maxterms**: **Opposite** — variable is complemented when its value is **1**.
- **Missing rows**: A truth table for N variables MUST have 2^N rows. Missing rows → incomplete design.
