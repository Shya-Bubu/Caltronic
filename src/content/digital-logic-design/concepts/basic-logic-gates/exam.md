# Exam Guide: Basic Logic Gates

## Typical Questions
| Pattern | Marks | Time |
|---------|-------|------|
| **Identify gate from truth table** | 2-3 | 2 min |
| **Draw transistor/diode circuit for a gate** | 4-6 | 5 min |
| **Voltage-level truth table** | 3-4 | 3 min |

## Common Mistakes
- **Confusing + and ·**: "+" is OR (not addition), "·" is AND (not multiplication). In Boolean algebra, 1+1 = 1, not 2.
- **Forgetting diode drop**: Diode OR outputs ~4.3V, not 5V. Diode AND outputs ~0.7V, not 0V.
- **Mixing up AND/OR output conditions**: AND needs ALL 1s for output 1; OR needs ANY 1 for output 1.
