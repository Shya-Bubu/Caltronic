## Common Mistakes
- **XOR vs OR**: XOR(1,1) = 0, but OR(1,1) = 1. Remember: XOR = "one or the other, but NOT both."
- **Counting NANDs**: Building OR from NANDs needs 3 gates (not 2). Don't forget the two NOT stages.
- **XNOR confusion**: XNOR outputs 1 when inputs are SAME (not different). It's the inverse of XOR.
