# Lesson 06 Synthesis

## Key Formulas

**Laplace Transform**: $X(s) = \int_0^{\infty} x(t) e^{-st} dt$ where $s = \sigma + j\omega$

**Transfer Function**: $H(s) = Y(s)/X(s)$ (for zero initial conditions)

**Key Pairs**:
- $e^{-at}u(t) \leftrightarrow \frac{1}{s+a}$, ROC: Re(s) > -a
- $\delta(t) \leftrightarrow 1$
- $u(t) \leftrightarrow \frac{1}{s}$, ROC: Re(s) > 0
