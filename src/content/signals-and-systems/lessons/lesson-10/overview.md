# Stability of Systems - Overview

This lesson is about deciding whether the system response dies out, stays bounded, or becomes unbounded.

The lecture note covers four pieces:

- stability from pole locations
- marginal stability and instability
- the series RLC example
- BIBO stability from absolute integrability of $h(t)$

The flow is:

$$
H(s) \rightarrow \text{poles} \rightarrow h(t) \rightarrow \text{stability class}
$$

and then

$$
h(t) \rightarrow \int_0^\infty |h(t)|\,dt \rightarrow \text{BIBO stability}
$$
