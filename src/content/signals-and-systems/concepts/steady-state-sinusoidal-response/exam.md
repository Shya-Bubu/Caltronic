## Exam Focus

You should be able to start from

$$
Y(s)=\frac{R(s)}{P(s)}+\frac{\frac{C}{2}H(j\omega_0)}{s-j\omega_0}+\frac{\frac{C}{2}H^*(j\omega_0)}{s+j\omega_0}
$$

and write the corresponding time-domain response

$$
y(t)=y_t(t)+C|H(j\omega_0)|\cos\!\bigl(\omega_0 t+\angle H(j\omega_0)\bigr)
$$

with a correct explanation of which part is transient and which part is steady state.

You should also be ready to state why the output sinusoid has the same frequency as the input, and why the Fourier-transform method gives the steady-state response directly.
