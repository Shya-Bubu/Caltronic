# Exam Focus: FT Properties

## Essential Properties

- Time shift: $x(t-t_0) \leftrightarrow e^{-j\omega t_0}X(\omega)$
- Freq shift: $e^{j\omega_0 t}x(t) \leftrightarrow X(\omega - \omega_0)$
- Scaling: $x(at) \leftrightarrow \frac{1}{|a|}X(\omega/a)$
- Differentiation: $dx/dt \leftrightarrow j\omega X$
- Duality: $X(t) \leftrightarrow 2\pi x(-\omega)$
