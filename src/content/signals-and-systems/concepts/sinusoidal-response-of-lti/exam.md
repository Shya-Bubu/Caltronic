# Exam Focus — Sinusoidal Response of LTI

## Common Question Types

### Type 1: Find Steady-State Output (6–8 marks)
Given H(s) and sinusoidal input, find Y(s), do PFE with complex poles, identify transient and steady-state parts.

### Type 2: Compute |H(jω)| and ∠H(jω) (4–6 marks)
Substitute s=jω in H(s), find magnitude and phase. Express steady-state output.

### Type 3: State the Sinusoidal Steady-State Result (2–3 marks)
"Output is sinusoid at same frequency, amplitude ×|H(jω)|, phase ∠H(jω)."

## Common Mistakes
- Forgetting the transient part (K₁e^(−t/τ))
- Not using complex conjugate pairs in PFE (K₂ and K₂* must be conjugates)
- Confusing ℒ{sin} = Aω/(s²+ω²) with ℒ{cos} = As/(s²+ω²)
- Applying FVT and expecting a nonzero result (average of sinusoid = 0)
