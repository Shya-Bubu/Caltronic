# Exam Focus: Fourier Series Properties

## Key Properties

- Time shift: $x(t-t_0) \leftrightarrow c_k e^{-jk\omega_0 t_0}$
- Differentiate: $dx/dt \leftrightarrow jk\omega_0 c_k$
- Parseval: Power = $\sum|c_k|^2$

## Application Tips

1. Use time shift for delayed signals instead of recomputing
2. Use differentiation property for signals defined by derivatives
3. Use Parseval to find power without time-domain integration
