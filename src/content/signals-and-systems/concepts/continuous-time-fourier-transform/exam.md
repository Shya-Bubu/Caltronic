- Forward: $X(\omega) = \int x(t)e^{-j\omega t}dt$
- Inverse: $x(t) = \frac{1}{2\pi}\int X(\omega)e^{j\omega t}d\omega$
- Convolution: $x*h \leftrightarrow X \cdot H$
